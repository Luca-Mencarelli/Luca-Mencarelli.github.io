Education

I received B.S. and M.Sc. degrees in Industrial Engineering and Management Science from La Sapienza, University of Rome, in 2010 and 2013, respectively. In December 2017, I received the PhD degree in Computer Science under the supervision of Prof. Leo Liberti and Prof. Claudia D’Ambrosio at LIX, Laboratoire d’Informatique de l’École Polytechnique, Paris (Thesis: The Multiplicative Weights Update Algorithm for Mixed Integer NonLinear Programming: Theory, Applications, and Limitations).

Professional Experiences

I have been Postdoctoral fellow at IFP Energies nouvelles (topic: Process synthesis and optimization: development and evaluation of approaches coupling simulation and optimization techniques) under the supervision of Alexandre Pagot. Moreover, I have been Postdoctoral fellow at CERMICS, École des Ponts (topic: Machine learning and optimization methods for long tail products supply chain) under the supervision of Yohann De Castro

Research

My research interests include:

  • Mixed Integer Nonlinear Programming

  • Portfolio Selection Applications

  • Chemical Superstructure Optimization

  • Nonnegative Matrix Factorization

  • Supply Chain Optimization
